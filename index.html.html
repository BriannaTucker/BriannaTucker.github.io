Dataset Varables Preliminary Explantion¶

In brief, there are 31 heros (playable characters) grouped into the roles tank, damge, and support in Overwatch. A player is on a team with five other players and this six-person team versus an enemy six-person team. The objective is to either attck or defend a point on a variety of 17 maps accomplished thorugh the use of your abilities. Each hero has their own unique abilities that can either damage enemy heroes or heal or protect friendly heroes. To conquer a point, all enemies must be off of the between whether it be from luring them off of the point or killing them on the point. Contesting the point increases your objective time. For a team on defense, the attacking team needs to be fought off until the game ends when the game timer finishes. For the attacking team, the point must be won before the time limit reaches the end.

A player can switch heroes at any point durng the match, and at the end of the match, a player's statistics are ranked with either a gold, silver, bronze medal to signify they either had the most, second most, or third most values for those statistics respectively. These statisitcs are eliminations (how many enemies you killed), objective kills (how many enemies you killed while contesting the point), objective time (how much time you spent contesting the point), damage (how much health points you deducted from enemies), and healing (how much healing you provided your teammates.
